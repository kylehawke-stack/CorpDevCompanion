## CorpDev Companion Design System

Theme: Bloomberg Editorial (dark, data-rich, authoritative)

Colors (5 total):
- Background: #0f1419 (primary dark)
- Surface/Cards: #1a2332 with border #2a3a4e
- Accent: #f97316 (orange) for CTAs, highlights, active states
- Text primary: #e2e8f0
- Text muted: #64748b

Fonts:
- Headings + body: Inter (font-sans)
- Data/metrics/monospace: JetBrains Mono (font-mono)

Card pattern: bg-[#1a2332] border border-[#2a3a4e] rounded-xl p-6
KPI values: font-mono text-3xl font-bold text-white
Section labels: uppercase tracking-widest text-xs text-[#f97316]
Signal badges: small pills with colored dots (green=positive, amber=warning, red=negative)
Pullquotes: orange left border, italic, tinted background, speaker attribution

Layout: max-w-7xl centered, 2-column grid for cards, 4-column for KPI strips
